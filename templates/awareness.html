Blood Donation Awareness
Eligibility Criteria
  • Minimum age should be 18 years.
  • Weight should be above 50 kg.
  • Donor must be medically fit.
  • Hemoglobin level should be normal.
Benefits of Blood Donation
  • Helps save lives during emergencies.
  • Improves blood circulation.
  • Free basic health screening.
  • Promotes social responsibility.
Emergency Awareness Tips
  • Always verify donor information.
  • Contact nearest hospitals immediately.
  • Maintain emergency donor records.
  • Use verified blood donation platforms.
